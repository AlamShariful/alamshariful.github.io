About Me

Self-motivated, goal-oriented, hardworking graduate student, enthusiastic polyglot programmer with more than a year of real-life experience in web development. Currently working towards my Ph.D. degree emphasize in Cybersecurity. My ultimate goal is to establish a research-based career.

I’m a Ph.D. student at Boise State Univesity(BSU). I have started my journey with BSU from Spring ’19. My research interest is mainly in cybersecurity with a particular emphasis on operating system security and hardware security. Currently, I’m investigating the possibility of establishing a covert channel using modern CPU features.

Born and raised in Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh. I have completed my bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh in 2014 and completed my master’s degree in Computer Science from Lamar University, Texas in Fall’18. Besides research, I like to visit new places, spend my spare time talking to my family and friends.
